Balla Battle (2025)

About

Balla Battle is a Wave Based first person shooter inspired by Left 4 Dead and Call of Duty: Zombies, with a corporate twist. Fight your way through the hordes of corrupt co-workers, and explore the sprawling halls of the Work Inc Office. Spend your hard-earned cash on weapons at the weapon printer, and buy refreshments to get yourself through to break time!

Drink Up, Lock and Load, your Job is on the Line.

My Contributions

Enemy AI

I was responsible for developing the enemy AI. I used the Unity Navmesh package to quickly implement pathfinding around obstacles, and developed a finite state machine to handle the various enemy actions.

User Interface

I was assigned to design and implement the various UI features of Work Inc, including the HUD, with features such as a health bar, ammo counter and wave display. I also designed the main menu, pause menu and settings menu, and implemented features for each. The settings menu featured a volume slider, as well as an adjustable look sensitivity.

Sound Design

I was responsible for recording and editing all vocal sound effects in the game, including enemy hurt sounds, and the voice lines for the vending machine. All sounds were edited in Audacity, and implemented using Unity's built in audio tools.

Where to Play

Work Inc was released on Steam, having been published by Demon Games, De Montfort University's Video Game Publisher. Work Inc was the first game to be published by Demon Games. Play the game here.
