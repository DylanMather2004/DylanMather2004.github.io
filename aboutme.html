About Me

Background

Hi! I'm Dylan Mather, a Graduate Game Designer from the United Kingdom. I started developing my own games in 2021 and have since gone on to graduate in Games Production at De Montfort university, and release my first game on Steam with my hobbyist group: Balla Games.

Education

I graduated with a Bsc in Games Production in 2025, achieving a first class honors. My final project was submitted to TIGA, and I achieved the accolade: TIGA Graduate of the Year: Game Design I was also nominated for Games Student of the Year at De Montfort.
